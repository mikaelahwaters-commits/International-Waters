Florence

May 28 – 31 · 3 Nights

Renaissance beauty at every turn. Florence is a city made for wandering — past marble cathedrals, across ancient bridges, and into tiny enotecas brimming with Chianti. Climb bell towers at golden hour, lose yourself in the Boboli Gardens, and end each night with gelato in hand.

Your Hotel
The Hoxton
2 Via Delle Mantellate, Florence · Confirmation #1062627089
Roomy King · Daily breakfast for two · €50 property credit · Upgrade upon availability
Residence Palazzo Ricasoli sits in a prestigious former palace and monastery dating back to the 16th century and faces the historical Via San Gallo in the centre of Florence. Careful restoration has turned the palace into a high quality modern residence offering excellent service and comfort.

Stop Three

Tuscany

May 31 – June 3 · 3 Nights

Rolling hills, cypress-lined roads, and the warmth of the Tuscan countryside. Your home base near Siena is a 40,000-square-metre estate where days unfold at your own pace — poolside mornings, wine-country afternoons, and candlelit dinners in medieval towns.

Your Hotel
Ca'Bianca Tuscany
Str. di Pieve a Bozzone 52/54, Siena · Reservation #6641354
Junior Suite · Daily breakfast for two · Pool, tennis, billiards · 40,000 sqm park
At Ca' Bianca you can choose the relaxation that most resembles you. Take a dip or an aperitif in the pool, walk in nature, play tennis or billiards. Breathe Nature, immerse yourself in the beauty that surrounds you and enjoy every moment. You are in a 40,000 square meters park among rolling Tuscan hills, good food and every comfort. We call it Dolce Vita, our all-Italian way of celebrating the joy of living!

Stop Four

Porto Ercole

June 3 – 5 · 2 Nights

Your final chapter unfolds on the Tuscan coast. Perched on a hillside between ancient fortresses, La Roqqa overlooks the sparkling Tyrrhenian Sea. These last two days are yours to unwind — beach club afternoons, long seaside dinners, and the kind of stillness that makes you never want to leave.

Your Hotel
La Roqqa
Via Panoramica 7, Porto Ercole · Confirmation #40443SG002691
Argentario Garden View Balcony King Room · Daily breakfast for two · €100 property credit · Upgrade & early check-in/late checkout upon availability
For total immersion into an authentic Tuscan experience, look no further than La Roqqa, nestled between fortresses on a hillside overlooking Porto Ercole. The hotel is the perfect spot for a secluded escape. A well balanced combination of mesmerising panoramic views, gentle escapism and local culture make it the perfect location for those in need of rest and recuperation.
